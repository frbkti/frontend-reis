About

Luxury hotel Bali comes with comfortable and modern accommodation in the Denpasar area, Bali. The hotel has a rooftop swimming pool overlooking the surrounding area, a spa and meeting/banquet facilities. The location can be reached in just a 15 minute drive from the Garuda Wisnu Kencana Cultural Park. Guests can access free WiFi throughout the hotel.The modern rooms with a comfortable design at Luxury Bali also have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Each room is also equipped with a seating area, safe, coffee and tea making facilities.
