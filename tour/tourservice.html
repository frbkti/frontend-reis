Description

Nusa Penida is a tourist spot that must be visited while on holiday to Bali. This tour package will take me to visit Angel Billabong, Crystal Bay, Broken Beach and Kelingking Beach. Broken Beach, a beach in the middle of cliffs 50-200 meters high. From the top of the cliff, you can see dolphins and stingrays and all the charm of the sea. The dolphins that appeared came from Dolphin Beach, which is close to Broken Beach.
